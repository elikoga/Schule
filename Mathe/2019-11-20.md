$$f_t(x)=t\cdot x^{-2}$$

$$F_t(x)=-t\cdot x^{-1}$$

$$F_t(1)=-1t$$

$$F_t(2)=-\frac12t$$

$$\begin{aligned}
A(t)
=&\int_1^2f_t(x)\,dx\\
=&\left[F_t(x)\right]_1^2\\
=&-\frac12t-(-1t)\\
=&\frac12t
\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}
&A(t)=8\\
\iff&\frac12t=8\\
\iff&t=16\\
\end{aligned}$$

$$f'(t)=4t^3-72t^2+228t$$
$$f''(t)=12t^2-144t+228$$


Hinreichend:

$$\begin{aligned}
&f'(t)=0\\
\iff&t=0\lor t=6\lor t=12
\end{aligned}$$

Notwendig:
$$\begin{aligned}
f''(t)&<0\\
f''(0)&=228\\
f''(6)&=-144\\
f''(12)&=228\\
\end{aligned}$$

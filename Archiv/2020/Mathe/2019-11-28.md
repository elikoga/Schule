$$f(x)=x+\frac{1}{4\sqrt{x}}=x+\frac14x^{-\frac12},x>0$$

$$F(x)=\frac12x^2+\frac12x^\frac12$$

$$\begin{aligned}
A&=\int_0^1f(x)\,dx\\
&=\lim_{a\to0}\int_a^1f(x)\,dx\\
&=\lim_{a\to0}\left[F(x)\right]_a^1\\
&=\lim_{a\to0}\frac12+\frac12-\frac12a^2-\frac12a^\frac12\\
&=1
\end{aligned}$$

$$\vec{n}=\vec{u}\times\vec{v}$$

$$n_1x_1+n_2x_2+n_3x_3=\vec{d}$$

Normalform -> Koordinatenform:

$$\vec{d}=\vec{a}\cdot\vec{n}$$

$$S_{x1}(\frac{d}{n_1}|0|0)$$

$$S_{x2}(0|\frac{d}{n_2}|0)$$

$$S_{x3}(0|0|\frac{d}{n_3})$$
